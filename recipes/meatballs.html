Cocktail Meatballs

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or a mix of ground beef and pork)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (plain or Italian-style)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped or grated
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey (or brown sugar)
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard (or yellow mustard)
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, milk, garlic, onion, salt and pepper. Mix until well combined. Form the mixture into 1-inch meatballs and place them on a baking sheet or tray.
  3. Heat a bit of oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the meatballs in batches until browned on all sides and cooked through (about 8-10 minutes). Alternatively, bake them at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes, until cooked through.
  4. While the meatballs are cooking, combine the ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, honey, mustard, garlic powder and onion powder in a saucepan. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Let it cook for 5-7 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Once the meatballs are cooked, transfer them to the sauce and stir gently to coat them evenly. Let them simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes to absorb the flavors
  6. Arrange the meatballs on a serving dish or platter, and serve with toothpicks or skewers.
